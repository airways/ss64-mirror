Stop-Transcript

Stop a transcript.

Syntax
      Stop-Transcript [CommonParameters]


Key
   CommonParameters:
       -Verbose, -Debug, -ErrorAction, -ErrorVariable, -WarningAction, -WarningVariable,
       -OutBuffer -OutVariable.

Example

PS C:\> stop-transcript
