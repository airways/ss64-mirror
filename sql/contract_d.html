DROP CONTRACT

Drop a contract from the database.

Syntax:
      DROP CONTRACT contract[;]

Key:
    contract     Name of the contract

Permissions reqired: the owner of the contract, members of the roles: ddl_admin, db_owner or sysadmin. You cannot drop a contract if any services refer to the contract.
