MonthName

Convert a month number to a descriptive Month.

Syntax 
      MonthName(number [,abbreviate])

Key
   number        The month number (1-12).
 
   abbreviate    True or False, abbreviate the month i.e. Jan or January

Examples

'Display a message:

WScript.Echo MonthName(12)
> December
