DEALLOCATE Clause

Deallocate an amount of space from table extents.

Syntax:

   DEALLOCATE UNUSED
   DEALLOCATE UNUSED KEEP int K
   DEALLOCATE UNUSED KEEP int M

Related Commands:

ALTER TABLE
